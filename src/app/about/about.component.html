About

Guardian Theater is a mash up of the Bungie, Twitch, and Xbox APIs.

Searching for a Guardian retrieves a list of recent activities that Guardian has participated in. Then for each activity, we to display all of the Twitch videos and Xbox Gamer DVR clips any of the participating Guardians recorded while the activity took place.

Guardian Theater was created by Chris Fried with invaluable assistance from Dustin Rue.

Why aren't my Twitch videos showing up?

The current solution for linking/unlinking a Twitch account from a Bungie account is to submit a request through this Google Form.

Why is the site so slow right now?

It could be the Bungie API, Twitch API, or Xbox API slowing things down. But it's probably Xbox. The Xbox API takes nearly a full second to respond to each request on a good day.
